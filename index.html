Health Care Services

The center offers comprehensive health care services, making it a one-stop destination for patients seeking medical assistance. Apart from diagnostic tests, they provide health check-up packages, vaccination services, and health counseling. This integrated approach allows patients to have a complete understanding of their health status and address any potential health concerns proactively.

Home Collection of Samples

Adona Diagnostic Centre recognizes that some patients may face difficulties in visiting the facility personally. To accommodate their needs and ensure accessibility to healthcare services, the center offers a convenient home sample collection service. Trained phlebotomists visit the patients' homes, collect samples with precision, and transport them to the laboratory for analysis. This service is particularly beneficial for elderly individuals, differently-abled persons, and those with mobility constraints.
