Home Sample Collection

Convenience at Your Doorstep today

At Adona Diagnostic Centre, we understand that your health is your top priority, and we are committed to making healthcare services accessible and convenient for you. Our Home Sample Collection service brings the diagnostics to your doorstep, saving you time and effort while ensuring you receive the same level of accuracy and reliability you expect from our certified and accredited labs.

How Does it Work?

1st Step

Schedule an Appointment

Call us or use our online booking platform to schedule an appointment for home sample collection. Let us know your preferred date and time, and we will be there as per your convenience.

2nd Step

Skilled Phlebotomists

Our trained phlebotomists will visit your home at the scheduled time. They will explain the procedure to you and ensure that you are comfortable throughout the sample collection process.

3rd Step

Collection Process

Our team will collect the necessary samples with the utmost care, following strict hygiene protocols. You can trust us to maintain the highest standards of safety and accuracy.

4th Step

Swift Transportation

Once the samples are collected, they will be safely transported to our state-of-the-art laboratory for analysis. Our efficient processes ensure that you receive the test reports in the shortest possible time.

5th Step

Expert Consultation

After the analysis is complete, you will receive your test reports within the specified time frame. If you have any questions or require further guidance, our team is available for a free report consultation.
