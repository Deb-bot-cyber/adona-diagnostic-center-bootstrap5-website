About Adona Diagnostic Centre

Delivering Reliable Healthcare with Utmost Care

Adona Diagnostic Centre, established in the year 2022, is a trusted medical diagnostic laboratory located in Kritti Basi Para, opposite SASA School, Khumulwng, West Tripura. The center is led by Dr. Chandan Debbarma, a dedicated CEO and Laboratory Supervisor, who ensures that the organization's vision of providing utmost service to patient care is upheld.

The center offers a range of essential healthcare services to its targeted customers, who are primarily patients seeking medical diagnostics. Adona Diagnostic Centre caters to common people, aiming to provide high-quality healthcare services at an affordable cost. The organization is committed to making a positive impact on the lives of its patients by delivering early and accurate reports.

The team at Adona Diagnostic Centre consists of five skilled and knowledgeable professionals who excel in their respective fields of expertise. They work diligently to ensure precision and efficiency in conducting medical laboratory diagnoses, catering to the specific needs of each patient.

The center provides comprehensive services, including medical laboratory diagnosis, health care services, and the convenience of home sample collection. This home collection service benefits patients who may face difficulties visiting the facility personally, ensuring accessibility to healthcare services for all.

With its core focus on patient care, Adona Diagnostic Centre aims to be a beacon of hope for individuals seeking reliable and affordable medical diagnostics in the Khumulwng area. The organization's commitment to delivering accurate results and fostering a supportive and empathetic environment has earned the trust and confidence of its customers.

For inquiries and appointments, customers can reach out to the center through various contact numbers, including WhatsApp. The organization can also be contacted via email at adonadiagnostics@gmail.com

In conclusion, Adona Diagnostic Centre stands as a reliable and patient-oriented healthcare provider in Khumulwng, West Tripura, committed to delivering utmost service and care to all its valued patients.
